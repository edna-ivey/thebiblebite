# The Bible Bite Brand Guidelines

## Product

The Bible Bite

## Tagline

Small bites. Big truths.

## Brand Rhythm

Quiz. Learn. Grow.

## Mascot Identity

Mascot name: Digby

Full mascot identity: Digby the Bite

Approved primary mascot: Bowtie Bite + Bite B

The approved primary mascot is the golden smiling Bite character with the
shallow upper-left Bite B nibble, small purple bow tie, and centered small gold
cross.

## Canonical Mascot Source

The canonical Digby implementation is the repository-native React SVG component
at:

`components/DigbyMascot.tsx`

This component is the single source of truth for Digby's approved primary
geometry. Do not create independently maintained static SVG copies of the same
mascot geometry unless a future integration milestone defines a generated asset
pipeline from the canonical component.

The canonical mascot must remain:

- repository-native
- editable
- vector-based
- scalable
- free of raster dependencies
- free of external image dependencies
- free of external font dependencies

## Mascot Personality

Digby is:

- curious
- warm
- eager to learn
- thoughtful
- friendly
- question-asking
- excited by Scripture discoveries

Core Digby energy:

> "Wait. Did you notice that?"

Digby should never feel:

- judgmental
- disappointed in readers
- shaming
- smug
- sarcastic toward users
- hyperactive
- preschool-only

## Signature Visual Features

Approved signature features:

- rounded warm golden Bite body
- shallow upper-left nibble
- approved Bite B contour
- expressive warm face
- rosy cheeks
- small purple bow tie
- centered small gold cross

The bow tie and gold cross are signature character details. The cross should
remain a small, centered highlight on the bow tie, not the dominant visual mark.

## Usage Principle

Digby should appear purposefully.

Do not place Digby decoratively in every section or every content card. Digby
should support curiosity, guidance, feedback, discovery, and brand identity.

Good uses include:

- welcoming readers into the daily Bite
- encouraging curiosity before a quiz
- guiding a reveal or explanation moment
- celebrating discovery
- anchoring brand identity in selected surfaces

Avoid using Digby as filler decoration, repeated card ornamentation, or a
substitute for clear interface hierarchy.

## Future Work

Additional Digby expressions, poses, seasonal treatments, social avatars, app
icons, and favicon exports belong in future integration milestones. They should
start from the canonical Digby source of truth and preserve the approved primary
mascot identity.
